About Our Project

📌 Problem Statement

Excessive social media usage may affect students' sleep quality and academic productivity.

🎯 Aim

To understand the relationship between social media usage, sleep patterns and productivity.

⚙️ Methodology

  1. Create questionnaire.
  2. Collect responses.
  3. Analyze data.
  4. Interpret results.
  5. Suggest healthy habits.

📊 Project Overview

This project studies how social media usage affects students' sleep patterns and productivity. Data was collected through a survey and analyzed to identify usage trends and their impact on student well-being.
