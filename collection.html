📋 Data Collection

Data for this study was collected through a structured Google Form survey conducted among students.

🎯 Survey Objective

The purpose of the survey was to understand how social media usage affects students' sleep patterns, study habits, and productivity.

📊 Survey Details

  • Total Responses Collected: 55
  • Target Audience: Students
  • Mode of Collection: Google Forms
  • Question Types: Multiple Choice and Opinion-Based

📝 Questionnaire Topics

  • Age Group
  • Daily Social Media Usage
  • Preferred Social Media Platform
  • Night-Time Usage
  • Sleep Duration
  • Sleep Quality
  • Study Distraction
  • Productivity Impact
  • Screen Time Reduction Efforts
  • Healthy Social Media Habits
