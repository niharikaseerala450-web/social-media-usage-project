A Study on Social Media Usage and Its Impact on Students' Sleep and Productivity

Community Project | Department of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning | Academic Year 2026-27

Project Overview

This project examines how social media usage affects students' sleep quality, study habits, concentration, and productivity. Data was collected through a survey and analyzed to understand current digital behavior among students.

Project Objectives

  • Measure daily social media usage among students.
  • Study sleeping habits and sleep quality.
  • Analyze the impact of social media on productivity.
  • Promote healthy and responsible digital habits.
